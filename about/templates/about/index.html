So , why is it required

“ Depression drains your energy, hope, and drive, making it difficult to do what you need to feel better. But while overcoming depression isn’t quick or easy, it’s far from impossible” I realized that it isn’t that tough to look at the positive side and can be done by reading related bogs , motivational stories , videos , maybe even songs and many people can be saved if given the proper guidance at the right time. The project is to make a MAZE MOODER , where depending on your mood you can play the game which is a maze game , where at each turn or specific points when the person reaches , they will be guided to a blog , motivational story and encouraging videos. The game would be made using python (we can use Pygame module of python)and the web application using Django . The Home page will have the register or login/Sign up option . After a user logs in , they can choose a mood either happy or sad . This would launch a maze game where the user has to cross the maze and in this journey , at turns or specific points , they’ll be guided to a motivational blog or story and videos or quotes. This way either they loose the game or win , they would definitely learn a lesson every time . If a user wins , then the win message will be flashed and if they lose , a similar message will be flashed. We don’t tend to read such stuff when depressed or sad but rather just go deep into the negative side , it’s either accidentally or on someone else’s saying that we try to pull ourselves up. So this application would help you relieve stress along with showing you the brighter side of life and leave you motivated every single time. “Depression is as common as flue” and this issue needs to be addressed .
